Business Areas

Supporting the field
across three pillars

Ultra-Fine Bubble (UFB) Solutions

A technology that stably disperses ultra-fine bubbles smaller than 1μm throughout water. With our in-house nozzles and engines, it is applied broadly across agriculture, livestock, aquaculture, cleaning·sterilization, and water quality improvement.

  • Higher dissolved oxygen
  • Improved cleaning
  • Better water quality
  • Reduced chemicals

Industrial Material Solutions

From industrial nonwovens·filters to functional composites — we propose materials matched to your required properties and process conditions.

On-site Technical Consulting

We solve problems that products alone cannot. We analyze your process conditions and quality issues to review the right materials, equipment, and application methods.

  • Feasibility review
  • Sample proposal
  • Process review
  • Field testing
  • Improvement proposal
  • Mass-production review
i-UFB Lineup

An i-UFB lineup
scaled to your site

From compact in-pipe engines to high-capacity multi-nozzle systems, every product is designed and manufactured in-house with precision stainless steel machining.

i-UFB Engine

UFB engines selected by treatment capacity

A 5-model lineup from 1.5i4T to 4i40T, selectable by treatment capacity. Applied to high-volume water treatment sites such as fish farms, agricultural water, and industrial cleaning.

  • 1.5i4T
  • 2i8T
  • 2.5i16T
  • 3i28T
  • 4i40T

i-UFB Nozzle

In-line UFB nozzles that connect directly to piping

An in-line type matched to 15A·20A pipe standards, easily installed on existing equipment. Choose from 5L·8L·10L·20L flow models and brass-nickel (BN) or SUS316L stainless (S) materials.

  • 15A5L
  • 15A8L
  • 15A11L
  • 20A20L

Consumer Line — FW Series

Everyday UFB for sinks and water purifiers

FW24 for sinks (24mm), FWP6A for water purifiers, and Bubblio shower heads — a compact lineup that brings the cleaning power of ultra-fine bubbles into everyday spaces.
