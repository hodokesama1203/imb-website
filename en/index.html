Ultra-Fine Bubble (UFB) Solutions

A technology that stably disperses ultra-fine bubbles smaller than 1μm throughout water. With our in-house nozzles and engines, it is applied broadly across agriculture, livestock, aquaculture, cleaning·sterilization, and water quality improvement.

  • Higher dissolved oxygen
  • Improved cleaning
  • Better water quality
  • Reduced chemicals

In-line UFB nozzles that connect directly to piping

  • 15A11L
