Industrial Materials

Industrial Material Solutions

IMB Co., Ltd. develops and supplies functional materials for a wide range of industries — automotive, electronics, environmental, and construction. We propose the optimal material based on your required properties, process conditions, and application area, delivering solutions that balance quality stability with production efficiency.

Key Fields

Key application fields

Automotive sound-absorbing materials

We supply sound-absorbing materials that improve vehicle NVH performance. We design material composition, thickness, and density to match the application area and required properties, delivering quality proven under our own ESION brand.

Industrial nonwovens · filter materials

We supply filter materials for masks, industrial use, air, and water-treatment filtration, along with industrial nonwovens. Under the DUSTLITE brand, we propose materials with the filtration efficiency and durability each use requires.

Functional composites

We design and supply composite structures that combine multiple materials according to your required properties.

Eco-friendly · recycled materials

We co-develop eco-friendly material applications based on recycled feedstock.
