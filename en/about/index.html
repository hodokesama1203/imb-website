About IMB

A company that solves industrial
challenges through technology

IMB Co., Ltd. is a technology-driven company that helps customers improve productivity, stabilize quality, and reduce costs through industrial materials and application technology. Drawing on experience accumulated across diverse fields — automotive materials, industrial filters, and ultra-fine bubble (UFB) solutions — we go beyond simply supplying products to deliver practical solutions tailored to real on-site conditions.

We first understand each customer's process and operating environment, then propose the materials, equipment, and services best suited to them. In doing so, we help our customers build more stable and efficient production systems.

A technology partner that
grows together with you

IMB Co., Ltd. aims to be a technology partner that analyzes problems together with customers, proposes workable solutions, and reviews everything through to actual deployment. We will continue to contribute to our customers' competitiveness through ongoing technology development and field-centered solutions.
