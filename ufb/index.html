The ultra-fine bubble
technology that changes water

Ultra-fine bubbles (UFB) are a technology that stably disperses extremely small bubbles throughout water. With our in-house developed UFB generating nozzles, engines, and application systems, IMB Co., Ltd. delivers solutions for agriculture, livestock, aquaculture, cleaning, sterilization, water quality improvement, and more.

Expected Effects

The change UFB creates

Higher dissolved oxygen

Raises the oxygen level in water, improving the living environment for organisms.

Improved cleaning power

Fine bubbles penetrate contaminants, boosting cleaning performance.

Better water quality

Used for water management in reservoirs, fish farms, and more.

Better crop growth environment

Improves irrigation water quality to support crop growth.

Better livestock · aquaculture conditions

Helps manage drinking and rearing water and reduce odor.

Reduced chemical use

Physical cleaning effects can lower dependence on chemicals.
